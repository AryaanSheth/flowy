capabilities

Built for speed.
Built for privacy.

Every decision made with one goal — transcription that just works, without ever phoning home.

100% Private

Audio never leaves your machine. No cloud, no servers, no telemetry. Apple's built-in SFSpeechRecognizer handles everything on-device.

Instant Results

Transcription appears in seconds after you release the key. Optimized for Apple Silicon — no model files, no setup, no warm-up time.

Push-to-Talk

Any global hotkey. Hold to record from any app — Slack, VS Code, Mail, browser — release to transcribe. Text injects into the focused window.

⌘ Shift Space

Custom Dictionary

Map word substitutions applied after every transcription. Teach Flowey your domain jargon, names, and abbreviations. Test live in settings.

Autostart

Enable launch-at-login and Flowey sits silently in your menu bar — always ready when you need it, never in the way when you don't.

Tiny Footprint

Built with Rust + Tauri, not Electron. The whole binary is under 15 MB. Zero background CPU when idle. Your RAM will thank you.

Ollama Enhancement

Optionally route transcriptions through a local LLM to clean up punctuation, grammar, and capitalization — entirely offline, zero cloud round-trips.

workflow

Three steps. That's it.

Up and running in under two minutes. No accounts, no model downloads, no configuration wizards.

1

Install Flowey

Download the .dmg, drag to Applications, open it. Grant microphone and speech recognition access when macOS prompts you.

2

Set your hotkey

Pick any global shortcut in Settings. Default is ⌘ Shift Space — change it to whatever fits your muscle memory.

3

Speak anywhere

Focus any text field — email, terminal, chat, docs — hold your hotkey, speak, release. Your words appear instantly.
