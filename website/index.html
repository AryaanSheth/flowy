Every decision was made with one goal: transcription that just works, without ever phoning home.

🔒

100% Private

Audio never leaves your machine. No cloud, no servers, no telemetry. Your words stay yours.

⚡

Fast Transcription

Powered by local Whisper AI running entirely on your CPU or GPU. No round-trips, no latency spikes.

📖

Custom Dictionary

Map your own word substitutions. Teach Flowey your domain jargon, names, and shortcuts.

🔑

Push-to-Talk

Hold any hotkey combination to start recording. Release to transcribe. Works in any focused window.

🚀

Autostart

Enable launch-at-login and Flowey sits silently in your system tray — always ready, never in the way.

🎯

Tiny Footprint

Built with Tauri and Rust, not Electron. The whole app is under 15 MB. Your RAM will thank you.

✨

Ollama Enhancement

Optional polish pass through a local LLM — punctuation, capitalization, and grammar cleanup with zero round-trips to the cloud.

Simple as 1 – 2 – 3

Up and running in under five minutes. No accounts, no configuration wizards.

01

Download a model

Grab a free Whisper model (75 MB – 461 MB) from Hugging Face. No account needed. One curl command and you're done.

02

Set your hotkey

Open Flowey's settings, pick any key combo that fits your workflow. Hold to record, release to transcribe — every time.

03

Speak anywhere

Click into any text field — email, code editor, chat app, doc — and hold your hotkey. Your words appear instantly when you let go.

Download a Whisper model

Flowey uses whisper.cpp compatible GGML models. Grab the recommended base.en model with one command:

curl -LO https://huggingface.co/ggerganov/whisper.cpp/resolve/main/ggml-base.en.bin
Model Size Speed Accuracy
ggml-tiny.en.bin 75 MB Fastest Good
ggml-base.en.bin 141 MB Fast Better Recommended
ggml-small.en.bin 461 MB Moderate Best
